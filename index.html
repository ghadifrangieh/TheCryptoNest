Crypto Nest Discord Server Guidelines

-Respect the Nest:

Honor the shared trading signals, market insights, and educational content provided by our esteemed traders. Altering, plagiarizing, or misrepresenting their insights is strictly prohibited.

-Educational Focus:

Understand that the content shared here, including trading signals and market insights, serves educational purposes only. Consult with a financial advisor before making investment decisions.

-Professional Engagement:

Conduct yourself with professionalism and courtesy in all discussions. Avoid derogatory language, personal attacks, or disruptive behavior that detracts from the learning environment.

-Signal Responsibility:

If you act on shared trading signals, do so responsibly and within your risk tolerance. Remember to assess and manage the risks associated with cryptocurrency trading.

-No Spam or Self-Promotion:

Keep the Nest free from spam, referral links, or excessive self-promotion. Genuine discussions and contributions are encouraged to foster a thriving community.

-Stay On-Topic:

Keep discussions focused on cryptocurrency trading, market analysis, and related topics. Utilize designated channels for off-topic discussions or direct messages.

-Privacy and Security First:

Safeguard your personal information and trading account details. Refrain from sharing sensitive data in public channels to ensure your privacy and security.

-Report Suspicious Activity:

Alert moderators promptly if you encounter suspicious or fraudulent behavior. Your vigilance helps maintain a safe and trustworthy environment for all Nest members.
